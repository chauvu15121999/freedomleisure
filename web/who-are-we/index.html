WHO ARE WE

Freedom Leisure Finance has a proud track record of over 15 years as a licensed lender. We can help you grow your business by providing your existing customers with an easy way to pay for your services, products and lifestyle they want through Freedom Pay and Freedom Money.

All of our services come at no cost to merchants. We charge zero merchant fees and require no hold backs.

For an instant cash top up of up to $1,000, Freedom Pay makes it easier for customers to pay for the things they want (and need) now, with breathing space to pay it off in even, interest-free instalments, with up to 4 months to pay. It’s BNPL for your customers, and no merchant fees for you!

For the bigger ticket items of up to $30,000, Freedom Money allows customers to dream big now through an unsecured personal loan with the comfort of competitive rates, flexible terms and a super fast approval time. View comparison rates. 
 
Freedom specialises in flexible and competitive loans in niche markets and our size guarantees that every customer receives equal attention. We continually measure ourselves by our standard of service and speed of approval. 

Mission: 
We support our customers every day with quick approvals, and the best personal service.

Values: 
The Freedom brand values are a set of guiding principles that shape every aspect of our business. They’re placed at the very core of our brand and are there to dictate our brand message, identity and personality.
